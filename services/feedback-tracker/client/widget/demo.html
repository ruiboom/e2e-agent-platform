Feedback widget demo

This page embeds the widget exactly as a real app would. Click the Feedback button at the bottom-right, leave a rating and a comment, and send it — then open the dashboard to see it arrive.

Served by the collector at /widget/demo.html. The widget posts to this same collector in open mode (no key needed). With auth enabled, add data-key="pk_…".

Programmatic submit (no UI) is also available: FeedbackWidget.submit({kind:"praise", text:"great demo"}).
